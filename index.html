Red River Gorge

WELCOME

Red River Gorge was almost turned into a lake for the purpose of flood control in the 1960's, but was saved by a protest hike by citizens and the Sierra Club. Red River Gorge is Home to an endangered plant. White-haired Golden Rod (Solidago Albopilosa) Red River Gorge has over 100 natural sandstone arches.
